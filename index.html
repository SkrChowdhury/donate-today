Our Donate Plan

Children’s lives are at risk as they wait for emergency relief. They need your urgent support for emergency supplies, nutrition, dry food storage, clean water and hygiene kits. Please donate.

Hygiene Items

Your donation can help us supply water and sanitation kits, including soap, water purification tablets, jerry cans, sanitary napkins, and buckets, to ensure that children and their families have access to clean water and hygiene items during and after the floods.

Clean Water

Your donation ensures our emergency relief response, including efforts for safe and clean water to prevent diarrhea and water-borne disease. It will enable continued support for nutrition, with therapeutic milk provided at Central Medical Stores and ongoing medical treatment by 149 medical teams who are already in the field.

Education For Children

Your donation also helps to ensure continued education for children, providing learning support like Emergency Education Kits for 6-to-10-year-olds so they can keep studying.

Thank You For Your Contribution

With your help, we can respond to these disasters and save lives. Thanks to you, in the last year alone we were able to provide more than 2 million women, men and children in 10 different countries with humanitarian aid and disaster response. When you give, you help us with both immediate response and risk reduction activities.
